Our Menu

Discover the taste of Korea!

Here at Moon & Rice, we don’t merely prepare meals; we create unforgettable experiences. The food served in our restaurant is made with utmost love and respect for the rich heritage of Korea. Every bite is an opportunity to feel thousand of years of Korean tradition.

Imagine savoring delicious, sweet, tender beef bulgogi or the unmistakable taste of spicy kimchi stew. Every dish is a combination of history, culture, while also maintaining a contemporary feel to it. The detail-crafted menu caters to everyone, letting you choose from a variety of dishes; from a comforting bowl of bibimbap to the trendy hot Korean fried chicken.
